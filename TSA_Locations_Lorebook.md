# Locations Lorebook

### The Sinclair affair - Locations Lorebook***  

***The Sinclair Estate***

Structure: A sprawling, two-story modern masterpiece designed with Richard’s architectural eye for "social exposure". The ground floor features an open-concept living area, a professional-grade bar corner for "whiskey-fueled confessions", a private studio for Richard, and a large gourmet kitchen with a central island table.
 
Private Quarters: The first floor houses three bedrooms. The master suite features an en-suite bathroom with a glass-walled shower. A second bathroom serves the other two bedrooms, including Mark’s.
 
The Grounds: A massive garden contains a large swimming pool, a secluded hot tub, and a detached sauna.
 
Voyeuristic Design: The bedroom windows are strategically positioned to provide clear, unobstructed views of the pool and hot tub area below, facilitating the family’s shared interests in voyeurism and exhibitionism.
 
The Cellar: A climate-controlled wine cellar beneath the house serves as a quiet, cool retreat for private conversations away from the rest of the household.
The Wright Residence
Aesthetic: Similar in layout to the Sinclair house but decorated with a more "trophy wife" aesthetic to suit Molly’s socialite status. It features higher-end designer finishes and "filler-enhanced" luxury throughout.
 
The "Stage": The pool area is the centerpiece of the home, designed specifically for Molly’s "risqué pool parties". It includes various sunbeds and lounging areas that act as a stage for Molly and Hellen to showcase their physiques.
 
Ross’s Nook: Ross maintains a cluttered, bookish study filled with art history texts where he "documents" the household's affairs as "research".
The Johnson Home
Focus on Fitness: Reflecting Abe’s career as a gym owner, this house replaces a traditional studio with a state-of-the-art, mirrored home gym that acts as a "social control hub".
 
The Backyard: While it lacks the sauna and hot tub of the Sinclair and Wright homes, it features a smaller, more intimate lap pool surrounded by high privacy fences.
 
Atmosphere: The interior is sleek and minimalist, often filled with the sounds of Annie’s provocative music or the heavy clinking of weights from the gym.
The Thompson Family Lake Cottage
Setting: A secluded, rustic-luxe cabin tucked deep in the woods near the lake, owned by Frank.
 
Features: The interior is dominated by a massive stone fireplace and deep leather furniture. Outside, a private pier leads to the water, and a wood-fired sauna sits right on the edge of the lake.
 
The Taboo Retreat: The isolation of the cottage makes it the primary location for the family’s most "depraved" and "forbidden" dynamics, far from the eyes of their suburban neighbors.  

***The Kuroki Residence (Zen Sanctuary)***  

Aesthetic: A modern, minimalist home inspired by Japanese "Zen" architecture. The structure uses clean lines, sliding Shoji-style glass doors, and natural materials like dark slate, light Hinoki wood, and bamboo to create a tranquil but highly sensual atmosphere.  

The Massage Pavilion: A dedicated room on the ground floor designed for Aiko’s professional activity. It features heated tatami-style flooring, dimmable recessed lighting, and a professional-grade massage table. One wall consists entirely of floor-to-ceiling sliding glass, offering a direct view—and easy access—to the private lake in the garden.  

The Ofuro Bathing Suite: A masterpiece of Japanese bathing culture. It is divided into a "dry" changing area and a "wet" room. The wet room features a deep, square Ofuro soaking tub made of aromatic cypress (Hinoki), which releases a woodsy scent when filled with hot water. Beside the tub is the traditional Araiba (washing area) with a low wooden stool and bucket, where Aiko often "assists" guests with their purification before they enter the tub.  

The Private Warm Lake: The garden is a secluded masterpiece centered around a small, man-made lake with constant warm water (mimicking a natural hot spring or Onsen). Surrounded by moss-covered stones, weeping maples, and stone lanterns, the lake is designed for "night-swimming" and intimate outdoor encounters. Its warmth creates a permanent mist on cool nights, providing a natural veil of privacy.

***The Sinclair Concierge Medical Suite***

Location & Aesthetic: Situated in an upscale medical building with its own private entrance to ensure the "discretion" of her high-profile proximity group. The interior is a masterclass in "Clinical Luxury"—polished marble floors, minimalist designer furniture, and ambient lighting that feels more like a high-end spa than a traditional doctor's office.  

The Consultation Studio: Madison’s private office features a massive, dark oak desk and deep leather armchairs. The walls are lined with her medical credentials and academic achievements, providing the "strict professional" backdrop she uses to disarm her "patients" before the teasing begins. A high-end sound system plays soft, neutral music to mask any noise.  

The Examination Suite: This is where the boundaries dissolve. The room is soundproofed and features a motorized, plush leather examination table rather than a standard crinkly-paper one. The lighting can be dimmed to a "diagnostic" blue or a warm, intimate amber.  

Voyeuristic Integration: In keeping with Richard’s influence, a discreet, high-definition camera system is integrated into the lighting fixtures, allowing for the recording or live-streaming of "sessions" to the Sinclair house’s private server.
