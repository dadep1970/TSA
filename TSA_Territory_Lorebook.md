***The Sinclair Affairs - Territory Lorebook***

**Oakhaven Falls**
 
The setting is an exclusive, wealthy enclave designed for visibility and social exposure.
 
Town Name: Oakhaven Falls
 
Oakhaven Falls is a secluded, high-end "bedroom community" catering to successful professionals like architects, doctors, and tenured professors. It is a town where "professional respectability masks private transgression".
 
Population: Approximately 4,500 residents.
 
Atmosphere: A "Clinical Luxury" aesthetic dominates the newer developments, featuring modern masterpieces with open concepts and floor-to-ceiling glass.
 
The Climate: Temperate, but prone to "cool nights" that create a permanent mist over the water, providing a "natural veil of privacy" for those engaged in outdoor activities.
Geographic Map & Key Locations
The Sinclair Estate (The Watchtower)
 
Location: North Ridge, overlooking the valley.
 
Environment: A sprawling two-story modern masterpiece. The master suite windows are specifically "engineered" to provide unobstructed views of the pool, hot tub, and detached sauna below. It serves as a stage for the family’s voyeuristic and exhibitionist interests.
The Wright Residence (The Social Stage)
 
Location: Adjacent to the Sinclairs, separated by low, manicured hedges.
 
Environment: Decorated with a "trophy wife" aesthetic and high-end designer finishes. The central feature is the pool area, designed for Molly’s "risqué pool parties" where she and Hellen showcase their physiques.
The Kuroki Residence (The Zen Sanctuary)
 
Location: The East Shore, nestled against the tree line.
 
Environment: A Japanese-inspired home using dark slate, light wood, and sliding Shoji-style glass doors. It is centered around a Private Warm Lake—a man-made onsen mimicking a natural hot spring, designed for "night-swimming" and intimate encounters.
The Johnson Home (The Power Hub)
 
Location: The Southern District.
 
Environment: A sleek, minimalist interior that replaces a traditional studio with a mirrored home gym. It acts as a "social control hub" where the clinking of weights and provocative music set the tone.
The Sinclair Concierge Medical Suite
 
Location: Downtown Oakhaven (The Professional Plaza).
 
Environment: An upscale medical building with a private entrance for high-profile clients. Inside, it features soundproofed rooms and a motorized leather examination table. To facilitate the Sinclairs' voyeuristic interests, a high-definition camera system is integrated into the lighting fixtures to record "sessions".
The Surrounding Wilderness
 
Shadow Woods
 
Description: A dense, ancient forest of oak and pine that surrounds the town, providing the physical barrier that keeps Oakhaven Falls secluded.
 
The Secret: It houses The Thompson Family Lake Cottage, a rustic-luxe cabin owned by Frank Thompson.
 
Stillwater Lake
 
Description: A deep, quiet body of water bordered by Shadow Woods.
 
Key Feature: The Thompson cottage sits here with a private pier and a wood-fired sauna right on the water’s edge.
 
Social Function: Known as "The Taboo Retreat," this is the primary location for the most "depraved" and "forbidden" dynamics, safely removed from the eyes of suburban neighbors.
